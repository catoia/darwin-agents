**Per order process (10 minutes):**

1. **Customer orders:**
   - "Maria" orders Oud Oil €69
   - Stripe charges her card
   - Email sent to you: "New order"

2. **You fulfill:**
   - Go to AliExpress
   - Find Oud Oil (€12)
   - Add to cart
   - **Enter Maria's address** (not yours!)
   - Pay €12 with your card
   - Confirm order

3. **Supplier ships:**
   - Supplier in China packs it

5. **You profit:**
