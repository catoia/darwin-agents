# Target List — 20 B2B SaaS Founders for Cold Outreach

**Criteria:**
- B2B SaaS, early-stage (0–5 employees, pre-Series A or bootstrapped)
- Likely doing cold email outreach (selling to businesses, not consumers)
- Active on LinkedIn or Twitter (so we can personalize)
- Email address findable (company domain or LinkedIn InMail)

---

## Where to Find Them

### 1. Product Hunt — Recent B2B SaaS Launches
Go to producthunt.com → filter by "Developer Tools" or "Productivity" → launches in last 60 days
- Look for solo founders or tiny teams
- Check if they have a "Contact" page or email on their site
- Personalization hook: "Saw your PH launch — congrats on [metric/feedback]"

### 2. Indie Hackers — Active Founders
indiehackers.com/products → filter by B2B → sort by "Recently Updated"
- Many founders list their email or Twitter in their bio
- Personalization hook: "Read your IH post about [topic]"

### 3. LinkedIn Search
Search: "founder" + "B2B SaaS" + "seed" or "pre-seed"
Filter: 2nd/3rd connections, posted in last 30 days
- Connect first, then message, OR use LinkedIn email finder tools
- Personalization hook: "Saw your post about [topic]"

### 4. MicroConf / SaaStr Community
Founders who speak at these conferences or are active in Slack communities
- Often list contact info publicly
- Personalization hook: "Saw your talk at [event]"

### 5. Twitter/X — #buildinpublic Tag
Search: `#buildinpublic B2B SaaS` → filter by accounts with 200–5k followers
- Many founders have email in bio
- Personalization hook: "Following your build in public journey"

## Research Time Estimate

- **20 founders = ~60 minutes of research** (3 min per founder)
- Prioritize founders who:
  - Posted about sales/outreach struggles recently
  - Just launched (they're in growth mode)
  - Are solo or 2-person teams (higher chance they're doing outreach themselves)

---

## Email Sending Batch Plan

**Week 1:**
- Day 1: Send to 10 founders (Batch A)
- Day 2: Send to 10 founders (Batch B)

**Week 1, Day 4–5:**
- Send Email 2 (follow-up) to Batch A non-responders

**Week 1, Day 5–6:**
- Send Email 2 (follow-up) to Batch B non-responders

**Week 2, Day 2–4:**
- Send Email 3 (breakup) to non-responders from both batches
