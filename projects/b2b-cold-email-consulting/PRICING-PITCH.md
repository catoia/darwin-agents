# Pricing & Offer Pitch

**Service:** Cold Email Consulting for B2B SaaS Founders  
**Target Client:** Seed to Series A B2B SaaS founders with <20% cold email response rates  
**Deliverable:** 90-minute strategy session + full email sequence + execution plan  

---

## The Offer Structure

### 1. Free 15-Minute Teardown (Lead Magnet)
**Price:** $0  
**Duration:** 15 minutes  
**Deliverable:** 3 immediate fixes to their current cold email  
**Goal:** Prove competence, book paid session  

**What they get:**
- Live review of one cold email they've sent
- 3 specific, actionable fixes (subject line, body structure, hook)
- Immediate takeaways they can implement today

**What you don't give away:**
- Full sequence strategy
- Follow-up timing
- Prospect list building
- A/B testing plan
- Scaling tactics

**Conversion target:** 30–40% of teardowns book the paid session

---

### 2. Paid Strategy Session (Core Offer)
**Price:** $200  
**Duration:** 90 minutes  
**Format:** Zoom call + follow-up deliverable document  

**What they get:**

#### During the 90-minute call:
1. **Full email sequence (5 emails):**
   - Email 1: Cold outreach (personalized hook + value)
   - Email 2: Follow-up #1 (Day 3)
   - Email 3: Follow-up #2 (Day 7)
   - Email 4: Breakup email (Day 14)
   - Email 5: Re-engagement (Day 30)

2. **Prospect list strategy:**
   - Who to target (ICP definition)
   - Where to find them (LinkedIn, Apollo, YC, etc.)
   - How to validate emails
   - Target list size (50–200 for first campaign)

3. **Execution plan:**
   - Week 1: Build list, send Email 1 to 50 prospects
   - Week 2: Send follow-ups, track replies
   - Week 3: Analyze results, iterate
   - Week 4: Scale to 100–200 prospects

4. **Metrics & testing:**
   - What to track (open rate, reply rate, booking rate)
   - A/B test ideas (subject lines, hooks, CTAs)
   - When to iterate vs scale

#### After the call (within 24 hours):
- **Deliverable document** (Google Doc or PDF) with:
  - Complete 5-email sequence (copy-paste ready)
  - Personalization guide (how to customize per prospect)
  - Prospect list template (CSV with targeting criteria)
  - Week-by-week execution checklist
  - Metrics tracking sheet

**Total value:** If they hired someone to write this, it'd cost $500–1,000. You're delivering it in 90 minutes for $200.

## How to Pitch the Paid Session

### Context: End of Free Teardown

After you've delivered 3 fixes in the free 15-minute call, transition to the paid offer.

**You say:**
> "Okay, so those are 3 things you can fix today. If you rewrite your email with those changes, you should see response rates go from [estimate] to [target, e.g. 12–15%]."

*Pause. Let them absorb.*

> "Now, that's the surface-level stuff. If you want to go deeper — full sequence strategy, follow-up timing, testing plan, scaling tactics — I do a 90-minute paid session where we build out your entire cold email system."

*Pause again. Gauge interest. If they're nodding or asking "what does that look like?", continue:*

> "It's $200. We'd spend 90 minutes together and walk out with:
> - A 5-email sequence (copy-paste ready)
> - A prospect list strategy (who to target, where to find them)
> - A week-by-week execution plan
> - A metrics tracking system so you know what's working
>
> Then I send you a deliverable doc with everything written out, so you can hand it to a VA or SDR and they can execute it. Basically, you leave with a complete cold email playbook."

*Stop talking. Let them respond.*

---

### If They Say "Interested, tell me more..."

**Reinforce the outcome, not the process:**
> "The goal is simple: by the end of our 90 minutes, you have a cold email system that's ready to run. You're not guessing anymore — you know exactly what to send, when to send it, who to send it to, and how to measure if it's working. Most founders I work with go from 5% response rates to 15–20% in the first campaign."

**Social proof (if you have it — if not, skip this):**
> "I've done this for [X founders / companies] — one guy went from 2 replies per 100 emails to 18 replies after we rebuilt his sequence."

---

### If They Say "What if it doesn't work?"

**You say:**
> "Fair question. Here's the thing: if you follow the system we build — the sequence, the targeting, the timing — and you're still getting <10% response rates after 50 emails, something's fundamentally off with your ICP or offer, and we'd need to revisit that. But the email structure itself? That works. The fixes I just gave you in 15 minutes will move the needle. The 90-minute session is just the full playbook version of that."

*No money-back guarantee needed — the free teardown already proved value. If they don't trust you after that, they're not the right client.*

---

### If They Say "I need to think about it..."

**You say (non-pushy):**
> "Totally fine. One thing to keep in mind: every week you're sending cold emails with a 5% response rate instead of 15%, that's 10–20 lost opportunities. So the sooner you fix this, the faster you start booking calls. But no rush — grab a slot when you're ready."

*Then send the calendar link in the follow-up email.*

---

### If They Say "Can we do it for less?"

**Option 1: Hold the price (preferred):**
> "I hear you. $200 is already below market for what you're getting — most consultants charge $500–1,000 for a full sequence + strategy. But I keep it at $200 because I can deliver it in 90 minutes and it's a win-win. If budget's tight right now, take the 3 fixes from today's call, implement them, and come back when you're ready to scale."

**Option 2: Discount if they're a great fit but genuinely broke (use sparingly):**
> "Tell you what: if you can't swing $200 today, let's do a lighter version for $100. We'll focus on just your first email and one follow-up — still actionable, just narrower scope. That work?"

*Don't go below $100. Free devalues the service.*

## Payment & Booking Process

### How they pay:
**Options:**
1. **Stripe payment link** (easiest)
   - Create a Stripe payment link for $200
   - Send in booking confirmation email: "Book your session here [Calendly]. Payment link: [Stripe]."
   - Only conduct session after payment clears.

2. **Venmo / PayPal / Zelle** (if Stripe isn't set up yet)
   - "Send $200 to [your Venmo/PayPal]. Once I see it, we're confirmed."

3. **Invoice** (if they insist, but slower)
   - Send invoice via Wave, QuickBooks, or Stripe Invoice
   - Net 7 terms max (don't wait 30 days)

**Key rule:** Payment before session. No exceptions. "Book now, pay later" leads to no-shows.

---

### Booking flow:
1. **They say yes on the teardown call**
2. You send calendar link (Calendly or Google Calendar booking link)
3. Booking confirmation email includes:
   - Session time/date
   - Zoom link
   - Payment link (Stripe)
   - Pre-session request: "Send me 2–3 cold emails you've sent recently so I can prep"
4. After payment clears, you confirm via email: "Paid — see you [date/time]!"
5. They send their cold emails 24–48h before the session
6. You prep for 30 minutes: review their emails, check their LinkedIn, draft sequence outline
7. Conduct the 90-minute session
8. Send deliverable doc within 24 hours
9. **Update `revenue-manual.json`:**
   ```json
   {"date": "2026-05-01", "revenue_usd": 200, "notes": "Strategy session with [Name] from [Company]"}
   ```

## Pricing Philosophy

**Why $200?**
- Low enough to be an impulse buy for a funded founder ($200 = 1 day of their time)
- High enough to filter out tire-kickers
- Delivers 5–10x ROI if their cold email improves from 5% to 15% response rate
- You can deliver it in 90 minutes + 1 hour prep = 2.5 hours total work = $80/hour effective rate

**Why not free?**
- Free advice gets ignored
- Paid clients implement because they have skin in the game
- You can't scale free consulting

**Why not $500+?**
- You're unproven at this stage
- $200 is low-friction for early traction
- Once you have 10+ testimonials, raise to $400–500

## Upsell Paths (Future)

Once a client completes the $200 session and sees results:

### 1. Done-For-You Sequence Writing ($500)
> "You liked the strategy session — want me to just write your entire sequence for you? $500, I'll write 5 emails, personalize 3 variants per hook, and deliver it ready to plug into your automation. You do zero writing."

### 2. Monthly Retainer ($1,000/month)
> "If you want ongoing optimization — I review your campaigns weekly, rewrite underperforming emails, and help you scale to 500+ outreach/month — it's $1k/month. Basically, I'm your fractional cold email guy."

### 3. Workshop for Whole Team ($1,500)
> "Want to train your whole sales team on this? I do a 2-hour workshop with your SDRs, walk them through the system, and leave them with templates + playbooks. $1,500 flat."

**Don't pitch these yet.** Focus on the $200 offer until you have 10 paid sessions under your belt.

## Key Metrics to Track

| Metric | Target | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Free teardown → paid session conversion | 30–40% | If <20%, teardown isn't valuable enough |
| Paid sessions booked per week | 2–4 | At $200 each = $400–800/week |
| Client satisfaction (would they refer?) | 100% | Ask after session: "Would you recommend this to another founder?" |
| Implementation rate | 80%+ | Did they actually use the sequence? Follow up in 2 weeks |

## What You DON'T Do

❌ **Don't offer "free strategy calls" with no clear next step** — that's a waste of time.  
❌ **Don't discount below $100** — devalues the service and attracts bad clients.  
❌ **Don't conduct paid sessions before payment clears** — no-show risk is real.  
❌ **Don't oversell during the free teardown** — prove value first, pitch second.  
❌ **Don't forget to track revenue** — update `revenue-manual.json` after every session.
