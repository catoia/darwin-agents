## Risk Mitigation

**If 0 replies after 7 days:**
- A/B test subject line (try: `One fix for {{Company}}'s cold emails`)
- Shorten email body (currently ~90 words, try 60-70)
- Test different hook type (all LinkedIn? all funding?)
- Expand prospect list to 50 (larger sample size)

**If replies but 0 conversions:**
- Review teardown call quality (were 3 fixes valuable?)
- Adjust pricing ($200 → $150? or add $100 lite option?)
- Improve paid session pitch (emphasize ROI more?)

**If spam complaints:**
- Stop sending immediately
- Review email content for spam triggers
- Verify prospect list quality
- Check domain reputation (SendGrid dashboard)
