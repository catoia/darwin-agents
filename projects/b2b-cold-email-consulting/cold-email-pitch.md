# Cold Email Pitch — B2B SaaS Founders

## Subject Line Options
1. Your cold email sequence teardown (15 min, free)
2. Why your outbound response rate is probably <5%
3. Quick cold email audit for [Company Name]?

---

## Email 1 — Initial Outreach

**Subject:** Your cold email sequence teardown (15 min, free)

Hi [First Name],

I noticed [Company Name] is targeting [their ICP, e.g., "finance teams at mid-market companies"]. I'm guessing you're doing some cold email outreach to reach them.

Most B2B SaaS founders I work with see <5% response rates on cold email — usually because the pitch is too long, too generic, or doesn't address a specific pain point.

**I'd like to offer you a free 15-minute teardown of your current sequence.** I'll look at your subject lines, body copy, CTAs, and timing — and give you 3 specific things to fix immediately.

No strings attached. If you find it valuable and want to go deeper, we can talk about a paid 90-minute strategy session ($200). If not, you still walk away with actionable fixes.

Sound interesting?

[Your Name]  
Cold Email Consultant for B2B SaaS  
[Calendar link or reply with your availability]

---

## Personalization Notes for Human

Before sending each email:
- Replace `[Company Name]` with their actual company
- Replace `[their ICP]` with who they're selling to (check their homepage or LinkedIn)
- Replace `[First Name]` obviously
- If they've recently launched on Product Hunt or posted about cold outreach on Twitter/LinkedIn, add a line referencing it: "Saw your launch on PH last week — congrats!"

**Tone:** Direct, no fluff, confident but not salesy. We're offering value upfront, not pitching a complicated service.

**Goal of Email 1:** Get them to reply "yes, let's do the teardown" or at minimum book a time.  
**Goal of Email 2:** Gentle nudge with social proof.  
**Goal of Email 3:** Create FOMO, leave the door open without being pushy.
