Budget Friendly Pasta Bake

Cost: $8-12 for 6-8 servings | Prep: 20 min | Bake: 30-40 min | Freezes perfectly

A good pasta bake is one of the best budget investments you can make in the kitchen. You spend 20 minutes assembling, pop it in the oven, and an hour later you have a bubbling, cheesy dish that feeds 6-8 people. These budget friendly pasta bakes are all under $12 total and reheat beautifully — making them great for meal prep too.

1. Classic Baked Ziti

Cost: $11.50 | 55 minutes | The essential pasta bake

Ingredients (serves 8)

  • 1 lb ziti or penne ($1.00)
  • 1 lb Italian sausage or ground beef ($4.50)
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce ($2.50)
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese ($2.80)
  • 1.5 cups shredded mozzarella ($1.80)
  • 1/2 cup parmesan ($0.80)
  • 1 egg ($0.20)
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning, garlic powder ($0.20)
  • Salt, pepper, red pepper flakes
  • Fresh basil optional ($0.40)
  • Bread ($0.80)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F. Cook pasta 2 minutes less than package says. Drain.
  2. Brown sausage/beef in a pan, breaking up. Drain fat. Mix with marinara sauce.
  3. In a bowl, mix ricotta, egg, half the parmesan, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  4. In a greased 9x13 pan, layer: 1/3 meat sauce, all the pasta, dollops of ricotta mixture, remaining meat sauce, mozzarella, remaining parmesan.
  5. Cover with foil. Bake 25 minutes.
  6. Remove foil. Bake 10-15 more minutes until bubbly and golden on top.
  7. Rest 10 minutes before serving. It cuts better and isn't scalding hot.
Nutrition (per serving): ~520 calories | 28g protein | 52g carbs | 22g fat

2. Cheesy Vegetable Pasta Bake

Cost: $8.20 | 45 minutes | Vegetarian option

Ingredients (serves 6)

  • 1 lb rigatoni or penne ($1.00)
  • 3 cups frozen mixed vegetables ($2.20)
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes ($1.10)
  • 1 onion, 4 garlic cloves ($0.70)
  • 1.5 cups shredded mozzarella ($1.80)
  • 1/2 cup parmesan ($0.80)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil ($0.40)
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning, red pepper flakes ($0.20)
  • Salt, pepper
  • Breadcrumb topping: 1/2 cup + 1 tbsp butter ($0.40)
  • Bread ($0.80)

Instructions

  1. Sauté onion in olive oil 5 min. Add garlic and Italian seasoning, 1 min. Add crushed tomatoes. Simmer 10 min.
  2. Add frozen vegetables to the sauce. Season well.
  3. Cook pasta to al dente. Drain, reserving 1/2 cup pasta water.
  4. Mix pasta with sauce. Add pasta water if too thick.
  5. Transfer to greased 9x13 pan. Top with mozzarella, then parmesan.
  6. Mix breadcrumbs with butter, scatter on top.
  7. Bake at 375°F for 25-30 minutes until golden and bubbly.

3. White Sauce Chicken Pasta Bake

Cost: $9.80 | 50 minutes | Creamy and comforting

Ingredients (serves 6)

  • 1 lb penne ($1.00)
  • 1 lb chicken breast, cubed ($4.00)
  • 3 tbsp butter ($0.30)
  • 3 tbsp flour ($0.10)
  • 2.5 cups milk ($1.00)
  • 1 tsp garlic powder, onion powder, Italian seasoning ($0.20)
  • 1 cup frozen broccoli ($0.80)
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella + 1/2 cup parmesan ($1.80)
  • Salt, pepper, red pepper flakes
  • Breadcrumb topping ($0.30)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil ($0.30)

Instructions

  1. Season and sauté chicken cubes until cooked through. Set aside.
  2. Make béchamel: melt butter, whisk in flour, cook 1 min. Slowly whisk in milk. Stir until thickened.
  3. Season sauce well. Stir in garlic powder, onion powder, Italian seasoning, and half the parmesan.
  4. Cook pasta to al dente. Drain.
  5. Combine pasta, chicken, broccoli, and white sauce. Mix well.
  6. Transfer to baking dish. Top with mozzarella, remaining parmesan, and breadcrumbs.
  7. Bake at 375°F for 25 min. Broil 2-3 min at end for golden top.

4. Budget Lasagna

Cost: $11.80 | 1 hour 15 min | The ultimate crowd feeder

Ingredients (serves 8-10)

  • 12 lasagna noodles ($1.20)
  • 1 lb ground beef or turkey ($4.50)
  • 1 jar marinara ($2.50)
  • 15 oz ricotta ($2.80)
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella ($2.40)
  • 1 egg ($0.20)
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning, garlic powder ($0.20)
  • Salt, pepper
  • Parmesan to top ($0.50)
  • Bread and salad ($2.00)

Instructions

  1. Brown meat with garlic. Add marinara. Simmer 10 min.
  2. Cook lasagna noodles. Mix ricotta with egg, Italian seasoning, salt, pepper.
  3. In 9x13 pan: thin layer of meat sauce, 3 noodles, 1/3 ricotta, 1/3 meat sauce, 1/3 mozzarella. Repeat 3 times.
  4. Top with remaining mozzarella and parmesan.
  5. Cover with foil. Bake 375°F for 40 min. Uncover, bake 15 min until golden.
  6. Rest 15 minutes before cutting. Freezes in slices perfectly.

  • 6. Mac and Cheese Pasta Bake: Homemade mac and cheese (butter + flour + milk + cheddar) baked with breadcrumb topping. The classic. $9 for 6.

Pasta Bake Tips

  • Always undercook pasta before baking — it finishes in the oven and will be perfectly al dente
  • Cover with foil for most of the bake — removes cover only for last 10-15 min to brown the top
  • Rest before serving — hot pasta bakes are molten inside; 10 minutes makes them cuttable
  • Freeze in individual portions — baked pasta freezes and reheats perfectly
  • Double the recipe — two pasta bakes take the same time as one; freeze the second
