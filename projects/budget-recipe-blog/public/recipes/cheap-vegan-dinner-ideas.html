Cheap Vegan Dinner Ideas

Cost: $3-8 per meal | Serves: 4 | Prep: 10-15 min | Cook: 20-30 min

Vegan food has a reputation for being expensive — quinoa salads and cashew cheese aren't cheap. But the original vegan staples — beans, lentils, rice, and vegetables — are the cheapest foods on the planet. These cheap vegan dinner ideas use smart shopping to deliver incredible flavor without the price tag.

1. Chickpea Tikka Masala

Cost: $5.80 | 35 minutes | Better than takeout

Ingredients (serves 4)

  • 2 cans chickpeas, drained ($1.80)
  • 1 can coconut milk ($1.80)
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes ($1.10)
  • 1 onion, 4 garlic cloves, 1 tbsp fresh ginger ($0.80)
  • 2 tbsp tikka masala spice blend (or: cumin, coriander, turmeric, garam masala, chili) ($0.40)
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil ($0.10)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro optional ($0.30)
  • Rice to serve ($0.60)
  • Naan bread optional ($1.50)

Instructions

  1. Sauté onion in oil over medium heat 6 minutes until golden.
  2. Add garlic and ginger, cook 1 minute. Add spice blend, stir 30 seconds.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, stir well. Simmer 5 minutes.
  4. Add coconut milk and chickpeas. Simmer 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. The sauce thickens beautifully. Season with salt.
  6. Serve over rice with cilantro and naan if using.
Nutrition (per serving): ~420 calories | 16g protein | 52g carbs | 18g fat

2. Black Bean and Sweet Potato Tacos

Cost: $7.20 | 30 minutes | Hearty and satisfying

Ingredients (serves 4)

  • 2 medium sweet potatoes ($1.80)
  • 2 cans black beans ($1.80)
  • 8 corn tortillas ($1.20)
  • 1 avocado ($0.90)
  • 1 lime ($0.40)
  • 1 tsp cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika ($0.20)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil ($0.20)
  • Salsa ($0.60)
  • Shredded cabbage ($0.60)
  • Cilantro + jalapeño optional ($0.50)

Instructions

  1. Cube sweet potatoes (1/2 inch pieces). Toss with oil, cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika, salt.
  2. Roast at 425°F for 20-25 minutes, flipping halfway, until caramelized.
  3. Heat beans in a pan with a splash of water and a pinch of cumin and salt.
  4. Mash avocado with lime juice, salt, and a pinch of garlic powder.
  5. Warm tortillas. Build tacos: beans, sweet potato, avocado, cabbage, salsa, cilantro.

3. Lentil Bolognese

Cost: $5.40 | 35 minutes | Nobody misses the meat

Ingredients (serves 6)

  • 1.5 cups green or brown lentils ($1.20)
  • 1 lb pasta ($1.00)
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes ($1.10)
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste ($0.20)
  • 1 onion, 2 carrots, 2 celery stalks, 4 garlic cloves ($1.20)
  • 1 cup vegetable broth ($0.40)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil ($0.40)
  • Italian seasoning, salt, red pepper flakes ($0.20)
  • Nutritional yeast optional (vegan parmesan) ($0.30)
  • Fresh basil optional ($0.40)

Instructions

  1. Finely dice onion, carrots, and celery. Sauté in olive oil 8 minutes until soft.
  2. Add garlic and tomato paste. Cook 2 minutes.
  3. Add lentils, crushed tomatoes, broth, and Italian seasoning. Stir well.
  4. Bring to boil, then simmer 25-30 minutes until lentils are tender.
  5. The sauce should be thick and meaty-looking. Add water if too thick.
  6. Season generously. Serve over pasta with nutritional yeast or basil.

4. Tofu Fried Rice

Cost: $6.80 | 20 minutes | Protein-packed vegan dinner

Ingredients (serves 4)

  • 1 block extra-firm tofu ($1.80)
  • 3 cups cooked cold rice ($0.60)
  • 2 cups frozen mixed vegetables ($1.60)
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce ($0.30)
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil ($0.30)
  • 3 cloves garlic, 1 tsp ginger ($0.20)
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil ($0.20)
  • Green onions ($0.45)
  • Sriracha to serve ($0.35)

Instructions

  1. Press tofu: wrap in towels, place heavy pan on top, 15 minutes. Cube.
  2. Fry tofu cubes in 1 tbsp oil over high heat until golden on all sides, 8 minutes. Sprinkle with a little soy sauce. Remove.
  3. In same pan, add more oil, stir-fry vegetables 3 minutes.
  4. Add garlic and ginger, 30 seconds. Add cold rice, press and stir-fry 3 minutes.
  5. Return tofu. Add soy sauce and sesame oil. Toss everything.
  6. Top with green onions and sriracha.

More Cheap Vegan Dinners

  • 5. Mushroom and Lentil Shepherd's Pie: Lentil filling with mushrooms in tomato sauce, topped with mashed potato. Pure comfort food for $8 total.
  • 6. Peanut Butter Stir-Fry: Any vegetables + tofu + peanut sauce (PB + soy + lime + garlic) + rice. $5 for four.
  • 7. Roasted Vegetable and Hummus Bowls: Roast whatever vegetables you have + serve over rice + spoon of hummus + drizzle of olive oil. $5.
  • 8. White Bean and Spinach Soup: Two cans of beans + bag of frozen spinach + garlic + broth + Italian seasoning. 20 minutes, $5.
  • 9. Vegetable Curry: Coconut milk + curry paste + any root vegetables or chickpeas. Endless variations from one formula. $6-8.
  • 10. Pasta Arrabbiata: Olive oil + garlic + crushed tomatoes + red pepper flakes + pasta. Vegan by default, incredibly flavorful, under $4 for 4 servings.

Cheap Vegan Pantry Essentials

  • Lentils (green, red, brown) — $1-2/lb, protein powerhouses
  • Canned beans (chickpeas, black beans, kidney beans) — $0.90/can
  • Coconut milk — transforms any dish, ~$1.80/can
  • Nutritional yeast — cheesy flavor without dairy, $5 for a big bag
  • Firm tofu — $1.80/block, 20g protein
  • Spices: cumin, coriander, turmeric, garam masala, smoked paprika — buy bulk if possible
