Affordable Taco Recipes

Cost: $6-10 | Prep: 10 min | Cook: 15-20 min | Serves: 4

Taco Tuesday is the most beloved budget dinner tradition for a reason. Tacos are quick, flexible, and let everyone customize their plate — which means even picky eaters get what they want. These affordable taco recipes cover every style and budget, from classic beef to creative vegetarian options.

1. Classic Ground Beef Tacos

Cost: $9.20 | 25 minutes | The gold standard

Ingredients (serves 4, 2-3 tacos each)

  • 1 lb ground beef (80/20) ($5.00)
  • 8 taco shells (hard or soft) ($1.80)
  • 1 packet taco seasoning or homemade ($0.50)
  • 1/3 cup water ($0)
  • Shredded lettuce ($0.50)
  • 1 tomato, diced ($0.60)
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar ($1.20)
  • Sour cream ($0.50)
  • Salsa ($0.60)

Instructions

  1. Brown ground beef in a skillet over medium-high heat, breaking it up as it cooks. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add taco seasoning and water. Stir well.
  3. Simmer 3-4 minutes until liquid is mostly absorbed and beef is saucy.
  4. Warm taco shells per package directions.
  5. Set up a topping bar: lettuce, tomato, cheese, sour cream, salsa.
  6. Let everyone build their own tacos.
Nutrition (per 2-taco serving): ~420 calories | 28g protein | 28g carbs | 22g fat

Homemade Taco Seasoning (Skip the Packet)

Costs pennies vs. $0.80-1.20 per packet — make a big batch and save

Mix Together (makes 3 batches)

  • 3 tbsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 1.5 tsp garlic powder
  • 1.5 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp oregano
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1 tsp salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper

2. Slow Cooker Chicken Tacos

Cost: $8.50 | 6 hours on low — dump and forget

Ingredients (serves 5)

  • 1.5 lbs chicken thighs ($4.50)
  • 1 cup salsa ($1.00)
  • 1 tsp cumin, chili powder, garlic powder ($0.20)
  • 10 flour tortillas ($1.80)
  • Toppings: cabbage slaw, avocado, lime, sour cream ($1.00)

Instructions

  1. Place chicken in slow cooker. Pour salsa over top. Sprinkle with cumin, chili powder, garlic powder.
  2. Cook on LOW 6 hours or HIGH 3 hours.
  3. Shred chicken with two forks directly in the slow cooker. Mix with the juices.
  4. Serve in warmed tortillas with toppings.

3. Black Bean Tacos (Vegetarian)

Cost: $5.80 | 15 minutes | Filling and surprisingly satisfying

Ingredients (serves 4)

  • 2 cans black beans ($1.80)
  • 8 corn tortillas ($1.20)
  • 1 tsp cumin, chili powder, garlic powder ($0.20)
  • Shredded cabbage with lime juice ($0.80)
  • Salsa ($0.60)
  • Pickled jalapeños ($0.40)
  • 1 avocado, sliced ($0.80)

Instructions

  1. Drain and rinse beans. Heat in pan with spices and 2 tbsp water. Mash lightly with a fork for better texture.
  2. Char tortillas directly over gas flame or in a dry pan.
  3. Toss cabbage with lime juice and salt — quick slaw in 5 minutes.
  4. Build tacos: beans, cabbage, avocado, salsa, jalapeños.

4. Potato and Chorizo Tacos

Cost: $7.80 | 25 minutes | Insanely good street food at home

Ingredients (serves 4)

  • 3 medium potatoes, small dice ($1.50)
  • 1/2 lb chorizo ($2.80)
  • 8 corn tortillas ($1.20)
  • 1 onion, diced ($0.50)
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil ($0.20)
  • Salt, pepper, cumin ($0.15)
  • Salsa verde or regular salsa ($0.60)
  • Cilantro, lime, diced onion for topping ($0.85)

Instructions

  1. Boil potato cubes 5 minutes until almost tender. Drain.
  2. Cook chorizo in a large pan over medium heat, breaking up, 5 minutes. Remove.
  3. In same pan with chorizo fat, add oil and potatoes. Cook undisturbed 3 minutes to get crispy edges.
  4. Add onion and cook 3 more minutes. Return chorizo, stir to combine.
  5. Serve in warm tortillas with salsa, cilantro, and lime.

The Perfect Taco Bar Setup

  • Protein in a warm pan or slow cooker in the center
  • Warm tortillas wrapped in a towel to stay soft
  • Toppings in bowls: shredded cabbage, diced tomato, shredded cheese, sour cream, salsa, jalapeños
  • Limes cut into wedges — lime on tacos is non-negotiable
  • Extra napkins (tacos are messy and that's part of the fun)
