Affordable Slow Cooker Meals

Cost: $8-15 | Hands-on time: 10-15 min | Cook time: 4-8 hours | Serves: 4-6

The slow cooker is a broke person's best friend. You dump cheap ingredients in the morning, go about your day, and come home to a ready dinner that smells incredible. These affordable slow cooker meals prove that budget cooking can be completely effortless.

1. Slow Cooker Pulled Chicken

Cost: $11.50 | 6-8 hours on low | Versatile — use in tacos, sandwiches, bowls

Ingredients (serves 6)

  • 2 lbs chicken thighs, boneless ($5.50)
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce ($1.80)
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth ($0.60)
  • 1 onion, sliced ($0.50)
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced ($0.20)
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika, cumin, chili powder ($0.20)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Burger buns or tortillas for serving ($1.80)
  • Coleslaw mix ($0.90)

Instructions

  1. Place onion slices on the bottom of the slow cooker.
  2. Season chicken with smoked paprika, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place chicken on top of onions.
  4. Mix BBQ sauce, broth, and garlic. Pour over chicken.
  5. Cook on LOW 6-8 hours or HIGH 3-4 hours.
  6. Shred chicken with two forks directly in the slow cooker. Mix with the juices.
  7. Serve on buns with coleslaw, or in tacos, or over rice.
Nutrition (per serving): ~310 calories | 28g protein | 22g carbs | 10g fat

2. Slow Cooker White Bean Soup

Cost: $8.20 | 8 hours on low | Thick, hearty, and filling

Ingredients (serves 6)

  • 1 lb dry white beans ($1.50) or 3 cans ($2.70)
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth ($1.60)
  • 2 cups water ($0)
  • 1 smoked ham hock or 2 strips bacon ($1.50)
  • 1 onion, 3 carrots, 2 celery stalks ($1.20)
  • 4 cloves garlic ($0.25)
  • 1 tsp thyme, rosemary, bay leaf ($0.20)
  • Salt, black pepper
  • Crusty bread to serve ($1.80)

Instructions

  1. If using dry beans, soak overnight. Drain.
  2. Add all ingredients to slow cooker except salt (salt toughens beans if added too early).
  3. Cook on LOW 8-10 hours or HIGH 4-5 hours.
  4. Remove ham hock, shred any meat, discard bone and skin. Return meat to soup.
  5. Smash about 1/3 of the beans against the side with a spoon to thicken.
  6. Season with salt and pepper. Serve with crusty bread.

3. Slow Cooker Beef and Vegetable Stew

Cost: $14.80 | 8 hours on low | Classic comfort food

Ingredients (serves 6)

  • 1.5 lbs beef stew meat or chuck, cubed ($6.50)
  • 1 lb potatoes, cubed ($1.30)
  • 3 carrots, 3 celery stalks ($1.20)
  • 1 onion, 4 garlic cloves ($0.70)
  • 2 cups beef broth ($1.20)
  • 1 can diced tomatoes ($0.90)
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste ($0.40)
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce ($0.20)
  • Thyme, rosemary, salt, pepper
  • 2 tbsp flour to thicken ($0.10)
  • Bread ($1.30)

Instructions

  1. Optional but worthwhile: Toss beef in flour, brown in hot oil in a pan before adding to slow cooker. Adds depth of flavor.
  2. Layer vegetables on the bottom of slow cooker.
  3. Add beef on top.
  4. Mix broth, tomatoes, tomato paste, Worcestershire, and seasonings. Pour over everything.
  5. Cook on LOW 8 hours or HIGH 4-5 hours, until beef is fork-tender.
  6. If too thin: mix 1 tbsp cornstarch with 2 tbsp cold water, stir into stew, cook on HIGH 15 more minutes.

4. Slow Cooker Chili

Cost: $12.50 | 6 hours on low | Freezes perfectly

Ingredients (serves 8)

  • 1 lb ground beef or turkey ($4.50)
  • 2 cans kidney beans ($1.80)
  • 1 can black beans ($0.90)
  • 2 cans diced tomatoes ($1.80)
  • 1 can tomato paste ($0.70)
  • 1 onion, 1 bell pepper, 4 garlic cloves ($1.20)
  • 2 tbsp chili powder, 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp smoked paprika ($0.30)
  • Salt, pepper, cayenne
  • Toppings: cheese, sour cream, green onions ($1.30)

Instructions

  1. Brown ground meat with onion, pepper, and garlic in a pan first (10 min). Drain fat.
  2. Transfer to slow cooker.
  3. Add all remaining ingredients. Stir well.
  4. Cook on LOW 6-8 hours or HIGH 3-4 hours. Stir occasionally.
  5. Taste and adjust seasoning. Serve with toppings.
  6. Freezes in portions for up to 3 months — double batch is worth it.

More Affordable Slow Cooker Ideas

  • 5. Slow Cooker Potato Soup: Cubed potatoes + broth + cream cheese + bacon bits + onion. 6 hours on low. Creamy and filling, $8.
  • 6. Lentil and Vegetable Soup: Red or green lentils + any vegetables + broth + curry powder. No prep needed, dump and cook 8 hours. $5.
  • 7. Slow Cooker BBQ Ribs: Baby back ribs + BBQ sauce + garlic powder. 8 hours low, then 10 min broil to char. $14 for 4 people.
  • 8. Slow Cooker Apple Cinnamon Oatmeal: Steel-cut oats + apples + cinnamon + brown sugar + water. Overnight on LOW. Wake up to breakfast ready, $3.

Slow Cooker Budget Tips

  • Cheap cuts like chuck, brisket, and pork shoulder are PERFECT for slow cooking — they get tender
  • Dried beans cost 70% less than canned — soak overnight, they're fine in the slow cooker
  • Frozen vegetables go in frozen — no need to thaw, add them in the last 2 hours
  • Always cook on LOW when you can — lower temp breaks down connective tissue better
  • Double recipes and freeze half — you get two dinners for the work of one
