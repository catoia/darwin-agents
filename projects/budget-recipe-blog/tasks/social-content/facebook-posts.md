## FACEBOOK ENGAGEMENT TIPS

**Daily routine:**
1. Check each group for new posts asking for recipe ideas
2. Answer 2-3 questions authentically (without links)
3. Like and comment on recipe posts from other members
4. Post ONE piece of content per week per group max

**What works best on Facebook:**
- Posts with a specific number ("5 recipes", "8 servings for $13")
- Cost breakdowns (people love seeing exact prices)
- Simple, conversational tone
- Ending with a question to invite engagement

**What to avoid:**
- Posting links without any context
- Posting in groups that explicitly ban self-promotion
- Posting the same content in 3+ groups on the same day
- Never respond to criticism defensively
