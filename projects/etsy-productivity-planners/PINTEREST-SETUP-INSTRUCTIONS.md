# Pinterest Setup Instructions
**For:** Human executor  
**Time required:** 30–45 minutes  
**Priority:** HIGH — do this BEFORE uploading Etsy listings (Pinterest traffic is critical in the first 2 weeks when Etsy Ads are blocked)

---

## Step 1: Create a Pinterest Business Account (10 min)

1. Go to **https://business.pinterest.com/**
2. Click **"Create a free business account"**
3. Sign up with a **new email** (e.g. use the Etsy shop email for consistency)
4. **Account name:** `ADHD & Productivity Planners`
5. **Profile description (copy-paste exactly):**
   > Printable planners designed for ADHD, PhD students, and freelancers. Digital downloads that actually match how your brain works. 🧠📋 Find us on Etsy.
6. **Website:** Add your Etsy shop URL once it's live (format: `https://www.etsy.com/shop/YourShopName`)
7. **Profile photo:** Upload `photos/adhd-mockup-1-hero.jpg` as the profile image (it's 2000×2000, perfect for a profile pic)
8. **Claim your website:** Once Etsy shop is live, Pinterest will let you claim it for analytics. Skip for now.

## Step 4: Join Group Boards (10 min)

Search for these types of accounts and request to join their collaborative boards:

1. Search: `printable planner` → filter by "Boards" → find boards with 5k+ followers → click "Request to Join"
2. Search: `ADHD organization` → find community boards → send a join request
3. Search: `grad school tips` → find community boards → request access  
4. Search: `freelance tips` → find boards with "collaborative" label → join
5. Search: `digital downloads etsy` → these boards almost always auto-approve

**Message to send when requesting access:**
> "Hi! I create niche printable planners (ADHD, PhD, Freelance) on Etsy. I'd love to contribute to your board with focused, high-quality content. My pins are all original digital products."

## Step 5: Engage (10 min/day for first week)

### Repin 10 popular planner pins (do NOT pin your competitors directly — repin adjacent content):
- Repin: productivity tips infographics
- Repin: ADHD organization tips
- Repin: grad school advice posts
- Repin: freelance business tips
- Do NOT repin: direct competitors' planner listings

### Comment on 10 pins with GENUINE value:
Good comments (add value):
- "This is such a practical tip! I've started using a similar brain dump approach in my ADHD planner ✅"

DO NOT spam: "Check out my Etsy!" in comments = shadowban.

## Why Pinterest for Etsy Planners?

- Pinterest is the #1 external traffic source for Etsy sellers
- 50%+ of planner buyers discover products on Pinterest before searching Etsy
- Pinterest pins have long shelf life — a pin from 6 months ago can still drive traffic
- The ADHD, PhD, and freelance niches are under-represented on Pinterest → lower competition
- Pins with high saves signal to Pinterest algorithm → increased distribution → free organic reach
