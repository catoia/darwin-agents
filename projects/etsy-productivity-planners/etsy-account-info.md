## Commission Structure (Portuguese Market)

### One-Time Fees
- **Store Setup Fee:** 16 €
  - Helps support new sellers and improve security checks

### Per-Listing Fees
- **Listing Fee:** $0.20 USD per listing
  - Charged when creating or renewing listings
  - Converted from USD to your payment account currency

### Per-Transaction Fees
- **Transaction Fee:** 6.5% of order total (excluding taxes)
  - Charged on each sale
  - Used to maintain platform security, buyer protection, customer support, seller tools, and buyer marketing

- **Payment Processing Fee:** 4% of order total (including taxes and shipping) + 0.30 €
  - Charged on sales through Etsy Payments
  - Percentage and amount vary by country

- **Currency Conversion Fee:** 2.5% of sales funds received
  - Charged if your listing currency differs from your payment account currency

### Optional Advertising
- **Offsite Ads:** 12-15% of order total
  - Optional for most sellers (but may be required based on sales volume over 12 months)
  - Only charged on sales generated through offsite advertising

## Profit Calculation Example

For a €10 digital planner sale:
- Listing price: €10.00
- Transaction fee (6.5%): -€0.65
- Payment processing (4% + €0.30): -€0.70
- Net after fees: **€8.65** (13.5% total fee)

If using Offsite Ads (15% on top):
- Additional Offsite Ads fee: -€1.50
- Net after all fees: **€7.15** (28.5% total fee)

**Key takeaway:** Budget for ~13.5% fees on organic sales, ~28.5% if using Offsite Ads.
