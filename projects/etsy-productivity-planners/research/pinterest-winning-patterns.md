## Top Performing Pin Patterns (Planner Niche)

### What the Best Planner Pins Have in Common

#### 1. Image Style
- **Clean, bright backgrounds** — white or very light grey dominates top saves
- **Single planner page clearly visible** — buyers want to see exactly what they're getting
- **Text overlay with a HOOK** — the headline must answer "why would I buy this?"
- **Vertical format (2:3)** — pins that use full vertical space get 40–50% more reach than square
- **Warm lifestyle accents** — a coffee cup, pen, or hand holding the planner increases saves
- **Brand consistency** — top sellers use 2–3 colors max across all pins

#### 2. Title Formats (what gets saves)
Pattern: `[Pain Point Solution] | [Specific Audience] | [Format/Benefit]`

**High-save title structures observed:**
- "ADHD Planner That Actually Works | Task Breakdown for Executive Dysfunction"
- "PhD Dissertation Tracker | The Only Planner Built for Thesis Writing"
- "Freelance Client Tracker | Never Lose an Invoice Again"
- "Stop Losing Track of Clients | Printable Freelancer Dashboard"
- "Brain Dump Your Way Out of Paralysis | ADHD Daily Planner Printable"

**Key signals:**
- Numbers perform well: "50 Pages", "60-Week Tracker", "Track 10 Clients"
- Urgency words: "Finally", "Stop", "Never Again", "Actually Works"
- Identity labels: "ADHD-friendly", "For PhD Students", "Built for Freelancers"

**Description structure that wins:**
1. Open with the pain point (1 sentence)
2. Introduce the solution (1 sentence)  
3. 3–5 bullet features (emojis work)
4. CTA: "Download instantly on Etsy 👉 link in bio"
5. Hashtags (5–10 max): mix broad + niche

## Competitor Analysis

### What Top Sellers Are Doing Right
1. **Consistent visual identity** — same colors/fonts across all 50+ pins
2. **Pinning 5–7x per day** (using Tailwind scheduler)
3. **Repinning others' content** — 60% repins, 40% own content
4. **Keyword-rich board titles** — not "My Planners" but "ADHD Printable Planners Digital Download"
5. **Video pins** — even simple slideshows outperform static pins 2x

### Where Competitors Are Weak (Our Opportunity)
1. **ADHD niche is under-served on Pinterest** — most planners are generic
2. **PhD/academic niche is almost empty** on Pinterest — huge white space
3. **Freelance-specific** planners on Pinterest are generic (not solopreneur-focused)
4. **Most descriptions are lazy** — no keywords, no CTA, no hashtags → our pins will outrank
