# Etsy Planner Niche Research
Date: 2026-04-28

## Research Method
Analyzed Etsy search trends, competitor listings, review patterns, and gap analysis to identify underserved niches.

## Top 3 Underserved Niches

### 1. **ADHD Task Starter Planner**
**Search volume:** Medium-high (ADHD productivity tools trending)
**Competition:** Low-medium (most ADHD planners are generic daily/weekly)
**Pain point:** People with ADHD struggle to START tasks (executive dysfunction). Generic planners don't address breaking down tasks into micro-steps or managing overwhelm.
**Differentiator:** Focus on task decomposition, 5-minute starter actions, dopamine tracking, hyperfocus session logs
**Target price:** $9.99
**Keywords:** ADHD planner, ADHD task management, executive dysfunction planner, ADHD productivity, neurodivergent planner

### 2. **PhD Dissertation Progress Tracker**
**Search volume:** Low-medium but highly targeted
**Competition:** Very low (no specific dissertation planners)
**Pain point:** PhD students need to track chapters, literature review progress, advisor meetings, publication deadlines, and defend timeline — but generic planners don't fit the multi-year, non-linear dissertation workflow
**Differentiator:** Chapter-by-chapter breakdown, literature review tracker, advisor meeting prep sheets, methodology timeline, defense prep checklist
**Target price:** $11.99 (higher education niche willing to pay premium)
**Keywords:** PhD planner, dissertation planner, graduate student planner, thesis tracker, academic research planner

### 3. **Freelance Client & Project Dashboard**
**Search volume:** High (freelancer productivity tools very popular)
**Competition:** Medium (but most are generic project trackers, not client-focused)
**Pain point:** Freelancers juggling multiple clients need to track project phases, invoices, client communication history, scope creep, and payment status — all in one place
**Differentiator:** Client intake form template, project timeline with milestones, invoice tracker, scope log, testimonial request template
**Target price:** $10.99
**Keywords:** freelance planner, client tracker, freelancer organizer, project management for freelancers, invoice tracker

## Next Steps
Design 3 templates (one for each niche), test pricing at $9.99–$11.99, launch simultaneously to see which niche converts fastest.
