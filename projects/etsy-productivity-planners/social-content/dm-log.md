## Notes on DM Handling

1. **Respond within 1 hour** on Facebook (algorithm penalises slow response)
2. **Respond within 24 hours** on Reddit
3. **Never send the Etsy link unsolicited** — only when someone asks
4. **Be personal** — use their name, reference what they said in their comment
5. **After purchase** — follow up once (after 3–5 days) asking if they have feedback
6. **Negative response** — if someone says "too expensive" → "Totally fair! It's also printable so you can reuse it — but no pressure." Leave it there.
7. **Question about product** — answer fully, don't be defensive, see it as product research
