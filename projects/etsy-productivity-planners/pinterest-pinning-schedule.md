## Why This Schedule Works

1. **Spreading pins across days** prevents Pinterest from flagging as spam
2. **7 pins per board** = enough to look like an active, valuable account from day 1
3. **Group board requests** are the fastest way to get extra reach in the first week
4. **Repinning + engagement** signals to Pinterest that you're a real user, not a bot
5. **1000×1500 format** gets maximum feed real estate on mobile (where 80% of Pinterest usage happens)
