## How to Update This Log

After each day, go to Pinterest Analytics → Overview → copy numbers here.

Pinterest Analytics location: `Business Hub → Analytics → Overview`

Metrics to track:
- **Impressions** = total times your pins appeared in feeds/searches
- **Saves** = times someone saved your pin (most important metric!)
- **Outbound clicks** = clicks to Etsy (requires Etsy shop to be live)
- **Followers** = profile followers gained
