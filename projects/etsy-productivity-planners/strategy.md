# Strategy: Etsy Productivity Planners

## The Opportunity
Etsy has a huge market for printable planners — daily, weekly, monthly, goal trackers, habit trackers, etc. Most listings are generic templates. There's an opportunity for **niche-specific productivity planners** targeting underserved personas: ADHD-friendly planners, PhD student research planners, freelancer project trackers, etc.

The key differentiator: **targeting a specific pain point with a specific solution**, not "2026 daily planner."

## Monetization Path
1. **Digital downloads** — $7–$12 per planner PDF sold on Etsy
2. **Planner bundles** — $25 for a 3-pack targeting one niche (e.g., "ADHD productivity bundle")
3. **Custom commissions** — once traffic is proven, offer custom planner design at $50/request

## What the Agent Does
- Research high-traffic, low-competition planner niches on Etsy
- Design planner templates (using code/SVG or instruct human to use Canva)
- Write SEO-optimized Etsy listings (title, description, tags)
- Create mockup images for Etsy listing photos
- Monitor sales and optimize underperforming listings
- A/B test pricing, tags, and descriptions

## What the Human Must Do
- Set up Etsy seller account (agent provides copy and guidance)
- Upload planner PDFs and listing photos to Etsy
- Finalize designs if agent-generated templates need polish (using Canva)
- Handle customer service (downloads, refunds, custom requests)
- Record revenue in `revenue-manual.json` after each week's Etsy payout

## First 3 Steps to $1
1. **Day 1–3:** Agent researches Etsy planner niches, picks 3 underserved targets (e.g., "PhD dissertation planner," "ADHD weekly focus planner," "freelance project tracker"). Designs 3 simple but functional planner templates (PDF format).
2. **Day 4:** Agent writes 3 Etsy listing drafts with SEO-optimized titles, descriptions, and tags. Human creates Etsy account, uploads listings.
3. **Day 5–10:** Monitor traffic. Agent tweaks tags/descriptions based on Etsy search analytics. Push for first sale at $9.

## Revenue Target
**$150 in 5 weeks** — 15 planner sales at $10 average.
