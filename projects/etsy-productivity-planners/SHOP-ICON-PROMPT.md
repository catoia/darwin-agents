# Etsy Shop Icon — AI Generation Prompts

**For shop:** Productivity Planners (ADHD · PhD · Freelance niche)  
**Required size:** 500×500px minimum (Etsy square format)  
**Must read clearly** at small sizes (~75–100px thumbnails in search results)

---

## 🎯 Recommended Visual Concept

A **minimal open planner/notebook** with a clean grid layout, paired with a small bright **checkmark or spark accent**, centered on a soft off-white or cream background. Flat icon style, 2–3 colors max. Feels structured, warm, and human — not corporate.

**Color palette:**
- Deep teal: `#1B6B6B` (primary — trust, clarity, focus)
- Warm amber/gold: `#E8A535` (accent — achievement, energy)
- Background: soft off-white `#FFF8F0` or clean white `#FFFFFF`

## 📐 AFTER GENERATING — WHAT TO DO

1. **Pick the best result** — ideally test 4–8 generations before choosing
2. **Resize to exactly 500×500px** — use Canva, Preview, or any image editor
3. **Save as PNG** (transparent or white background both work on Etsy)
4. **Upload** in Etsy Shop Manager → Shop settings → Shop icon

### Quick resize in Canva (free):
1. Go to canva.com → Create design → Custom size → 500×500 px
2. Upload your generated image → drag it to fill the canvas
3. Download as PNG

## 💡 WHAT MAKES A GOOD ETSY SHOP ICON

- **Reads at tiny size** — most buyers see it as a ~75px thumbnail next to your listings
- **2 colors max** — more colors = muddy at small sizes
- **No text** — shop name is already shown next to it on Etsy, don't double up
- **Unique** — avoid generic clipart style; geometric + flat stands out
- **Warm, not cold** — buyers trust shops that feel human, not corporate

The teal + gold palette signals: organized, premium, trustworthy — a strong match for the
ADHD, PhD, and Freelance audiences who are spending money on tools to get their life together.
