# Freelance Client & Project Dashboard — Design Specification

## Format
- **Size:** US Letter (8.5" × 11")
- **Pages:** 55 sheets (printable)
- **Color:** Black & white with professional green accents
- **Font:** Modern sans-serif (Inter or Roboto), 10-12pt

## Page Templates (55 total)

### Invoice Tracker (10 pages)
- Invoice #, Client, Amount, Date sent, Date due, Date paid
- 10 invoices per page, 100 total

### Weekly Freelance Dashboard (10 pages)
- Hours logged by client
- Revenue earned this week
- Upcoming deadlines
- Tasks blocked/waiting on client

### Testimonial Request Template (3 pages)
- Pre-written email template
- Best time to ask (right after win)
- Where to use testimonials

## Key Features
- **Client-centric organization** — not project-centric
- **Scope creep protection** — explicit log for out-of-scope requests
- **Cash flow focus** — invoice tracking front and center
- **Testimonial prompts** — builds reputation systematically
