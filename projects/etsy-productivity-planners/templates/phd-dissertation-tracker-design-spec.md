# PhD Dissertation Progress Tracker — Design Specification

## Format
- **Size:** US Letter (8.5" × 11")
- **Pages:** 60 sheets (printable)
- **Color:** Black & white with academic blue accents
- **Font:** Professional serif (Crimson Text or Lora), 10-12pt

## Page Templates (60 total)

### Literature Review Tracker (20 pages)
- 4 entries per page, 80 sources total
- Fields: Author, Year, Key argument, Where I'll cite it, Notes

### Advisor Meeting Prep (12 pages)
- Pre-meeting agenda template
- Questions to ask
- Progress update summary
- Post-meeting action items

### Defense Preparation Checklist (5 pages)
- 90-day defense countdown
- Presentation outline
- Anticipated questions
- Practice schedule

### Weekly Dissertation Log (4 pages)
- Hours logged this week
- Pages written
- Obstacles encountered
- Next week's priorities

## Key Features
- **Chapter-centric workflow** — matches how dissertations are actually written
- **Literature integration** — prevents lost citations
- **Advisor accountability** — structured meeting logs
- **Defense prep** — starts early, reduces panic
