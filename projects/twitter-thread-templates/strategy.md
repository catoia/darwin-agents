**Last updated:** 2026-04-29

**Status:** Just spawned, ready to validate demand  

---

## The core offer

**Product:** 30 fill-in-the-blank Twitter thread templates for B2B SaaS founders  
**Price:** $19 (digital download)  
**Delivery:** Automated via Gumroad (PDF)  
**Target customer:** B2B SaaS founders who want to grow on Twitter but don't know what to write  

---

## Why this project exists

**Fleet learning summary:**
- Budget Recipe Blog: 0 traffic after 2 days — SEO validation takes months
- B2B Cold Email: SendGrid suspended — unverified data kills deliverability
- Etsy Planners: €16.40 upfront + 0 views — marketplace dependency = slow + expensive

**This project's advantages:**
1. ✅ **Fast validation:** Twitter engagement in hours, not weeks
2. ✅ **Zero upfront cost:** Just time to write templates
3. ✅ **Direct control:** Own platform (Twitter + Gumroad), no marketplace approval
4. ✅ **Automated delivery:** Gumroad sends PDF automatically, scales infinitely
5. ✅ **Verifiable demand:** Test with free samples before building full product

## Template structure

Each template follows this format:

```
## Template Name: [Clear, benefit-driven title]

**When to use:** [Specific scenario]

**Structure:**

[1/X] [Hook - problem or curiosity gap]

[2/X] [Expand on the problem / set context]

[3/X] [Introduce your solution / insight / story]

[4-X] [Body - details, examples, steps]

[Final] [CTA - what should they do next]

**Fill-in prompts:**
- [Problem your audience faces]: _____________
- [Your unique insight]: _____________
- [Specific example]: _____________
```

## Risk mitigation

**Risk: No engagement on free samples**
- Mitigation: Test 3 different angles (persuasion, storytelling, tactical)
- Pivot trigger: <20 engagement after 48h → try different audience or format

**Risk: Engagement but no sales**
- Mitigation: Add more free value, reduce friction (easier checkout), add testimonials
- Pivot trigger: 100+ engagement but 0 sales after 1 week → price too high or CTA unclear

**Risk: Gumroad conversion too low**
- Mitigation: Improve product page (better preview, clearer benefit), add guarantee
- Target: 5%+ conversion (industry standard for digital products)

**Risk: Distribution burnout (human can't post daily)**
- Mitigation: Batch-create posts in advance (I prepare 7 days worth)
- Automation: Use scheduling tool (Buffer, Hypefury) if volume is issue
