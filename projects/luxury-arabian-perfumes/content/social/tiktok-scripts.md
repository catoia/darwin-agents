## WEEKLY TRENDING AUDIO CHECKLIST

Every Monday, do this 5-minute task:
1. Open TikTok → Discover → tap 🔍 → "Trending Sounds"
2. Find 5-10 sounds with the 📈 arrow (trending)
3. Save them to your TikTok drafts immediately
4. Use in videos that week before they peak

**Sound types that work for luxury/fragrance:**
- Cinematic/orchestral (for storytelling videos)
- Aesthetic/chill (for lifestyle/morning routine)
- ASMR/ambient (for tutorials/product demos)
- Upbeat trending pop (for educational quick-cuts)
- Emotional/instrumental (for brand story videos)
