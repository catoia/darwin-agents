## 🔬 HASHTAG RESEARCH PROCESS (Ongoing)

### Monthly Hashtag Audit (30 min, first Sunday of month)
1. Open Instagram, search each primary hashtag
2. Check: posts with that tag still active? Growing or declining?
3. Check: are top posts from big brands (harder to compete) or smaller accounts?
4. Look for **new** related hashtags in top posts — add them to your rotation

### Finding New Niche Hashtags
1. Go to your best-performing post
2. Click on any hashtag → see what other posts appear
3. Look at those posts → what hashtags do THEY use?
4. Find the ones with 10K-500K posts (sweet spot for niche reach)
5. Add to your rotation

### Portuguese-Specific Hashtag Monitoring
Check monthly and engage with all posts:
- #PerfumePortugal
- #FragranciaPortugal  
- #PerfumeSemAlcool
