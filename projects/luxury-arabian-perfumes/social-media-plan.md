- **Voice:** Sophisticated, mystical, exclusive — not salesy

- **Promise:** "Ancient rituals. Modern luxury."
- **Differentiator:** Authentic Arabian fragrances in Portugal — rare, premium, exotic

### Platform Roles
| Platform | Purpose | Posting Frequency | Time to ROI |
|----------|----------|-------------------|-------------|
| **Instagram** | Brand home + sales | 1 feed + daily stories | 2-3 weeks |

## 📅 30-Day Content Calendar Overview

### Week 1 (Days 1-7): **ESTABLISH**
_Goal: Set the aesthetic. Introduce the world of Arabian fragrance._
- Launch feed with 3-4 hero posts
- Education content: What is Oud? What is Attar? What is Bakhoor?
- Stories: polls + Q&A to build engagement habit
- TikTok: 2 educational videos
- Pinterest: Create all boards, post 10 pins

### Week 2 (Days 8-14): **EDUCATE**
_Goal: Build authority. Become the go-to source for Arabian perfume knowledge in PT_
- Behind-the-scenes content (sourcing, quality)
- "How to choose your first Oud" carousel
- Stories: daily fragrance tips
- TikTok: 3 educational + 1 unboxing
- Pinterest: 10 more pins

### Week 3 (Days 15-21): **ENGAGE**
_Goal: Community. Get comments, shares, saves. Push to website._
- User-generated content hooks (ask for scent stories)
- Comparison posts (synthetic vs natural)
- Stories: link to website product pages
- TikTok: lifestyle + trending audio
- Pinterest: 10 more pins

### Week 4 (Days 22-30): **CONVERT**
_Goal: First sales. Drive traffic to site._
- Social proof posts (early customer feedback if any)
- Limited stock urgency (truthful)
- "How to order" story series
- TikTok: order process + testimonials
- Direct DM outreach to warm followers

## 🔑 Keys to Success

1. **Consistency beats perfection** — post every day, even if imperfect
2. **Respond to every comment within 2 hours** — the algorithm rewards this
3. **Save = best metric** — Instagram favors posts with high saves (educational > pretty)
4. **TikTok is your fastest growth lever** — one viral video = 10,000+ impressions
5. **Pinterest is a long game** — set it up correctly once, it compounds over months
6. **Story polls daily** — forces interaction, builds algorithmic momentum
7. **Quality over quantity on TikTok** — 1 great video > 5 mediocre ones
